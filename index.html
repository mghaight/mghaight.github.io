Haigmac is the personal webpage of Mackenzie Haight. Truncating and rearranging his names in the title is just one example of how Mac is creative and forward thinking. Mac is passionate about building things as long as those things are web applications or mobile applications. At the College of William & Mary, Mac studies Computer Science and competes on the varsity Cross Country and Track & Field teams. Having lived in Virginia for all of his life, Mac hopes to travel often after graduation to fit in with the crowd of interesting and cultured twenty-somethings he hangs around. Such destinations include: Portland, OR; Boulder, CO; and London, GBR. Aside from travel, code and running, Mac is interested in art especially mediums such as photography and mixed media. If you see anything here that fills you with joy, sadness, rage or any other emotion please contact Mac here.
